About Me

When I was a kid, one of my favorite games was WarioWare: D.I.Y. for the Nintendo DS. It allows the player to create simple 5-10 second long microgames using basic programming commands, like "move x or y" and when ___ happens do ___. I made games like making mario jump to get a coin while moving, or making a space ship move at the right time to dodge an asteroid.

Now, 10 years later, I'm a student at Thaddeus Stevens College of Technology studying computer software engineering. All that experience that I had learning how to think programatically is now being applied, only instead of making microgames I'm making websites.
